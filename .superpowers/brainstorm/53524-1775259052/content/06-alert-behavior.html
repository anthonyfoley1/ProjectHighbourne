How should the dashboard surface "look at this NOW" moments?

You said you want outliers and opportunistic trades to catch your attention. How aggressive should the alerting be?

A

Visual Highlighting Only

Rows in the screener glow or pulse when multiple signals converge (e.g., RV z-score < -2 AND RSI < 30). You notice them when you're looking at the dashboard. No notifications.

B

Alert Banner / Panel

A dedicated "Alerts" section at the top of the dashboard that lists stocks crossing key thresholds. Like a Bloomberg alert ticker — always visible, updates on refresh.

C

Just the Data — I'll Spot It

Good sorting, filtering, and color-coded columns are enough. You're a PM — you know what to look for. Don't over-automate the scanning process.
