Which technical indicators matter most to you?

Pick the ones you'd actually use to decide "this is oversold, time to look closer." We can always add more later.

1

RSI (Relative Strength Index)

Classic overbought (>70) / oversold (<30) oscillator. Simple, widely used, clear thresholds.

2

Moving Average Crossovers

50-day vs 200-day SMA (golden cross / death cross). Shows trend direction and momentum shifts.

3

Bollinger Bands (on Price)

Price relative to its own mean ± 2σ. You already use this concept on ratios — same logic applied to price.

4

MACD

Momentum and trend-following. Signal line crossovers and histogram divergences catch turning points.

5

Volume Profile / VWAP

Volume-weighted context — confirms whether price moves have conviction behind them.

6

ATR (Average True Range)

Volatility measure — useful for position sizing and identifying when a stock is moving unusually.
