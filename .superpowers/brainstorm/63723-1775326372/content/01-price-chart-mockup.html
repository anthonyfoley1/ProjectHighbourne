WHAT'S NEW

  • Bollinger Bands — 20-period SMA ± 2 std dev (cyan dashed lines + fill). Auto-scales period to time window.
  • 50/200 SMA overlays — on the main price chart (orange/red dashed), not a separate chart.
  • Toggle checkboxes — turn overlays on/off without cluttering the chart.
  • GC/DC markers — golden cross and death cross annotations where MAs cross.
  • Current price badge — cyan label on the right edge.
  • Area fill — cyan area under price line (like the Capital IQ screenshot).

BBand auto-scaling: 1D-1M → period=10, σ=1.5 | 3M-1Y → period=20, σ=2.0 | 2Y+ → period=50, σ=2.0
